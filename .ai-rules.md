# AI Governance & SEO Standards for arpitsharma.me

This file contains mandatory instructions for any AI agent (LLM, Copilot, or Autonomous Agent) operating within this repository.

## 1. SEO & Metadata Standards
- **Every Blog Post** must include a `description` field in its front-matter (YAML). The description should be 1-2 sentences, high-impact, and optimized for search snippets.
- **Consulting Keywords:** When appropriate, subtly include keywords like *Engineering Executive*, *Fintech Consulting*, *Fractional CTO*, and *AI Infrastructure Architect*.
- **Truncation:** All blog posts must include the `<!-- truncate -->` marker after the first 2-3 paragraphs to ensure the blog list page remains clean.

## 2. Global Identity
- The site follows a **Premium/Luxury Engineering** aesthetic (Dark mode, Gold accents).
- Descriptions should emphasize **scale** (99.999% uptime), **complexity** ($6M revenue recovered), and **leadership philosophy** (decision-making under pressure).

## 3. Deployment Safety
- Before pushing changes, ensure the `docusaurus.config.js` is linted and free of syntax errors.
- Always check that internal links follow the Docusaurus format (e.g., `/blog`, `/docs/intro`).

## 4. AI-Crawler Optimization
- Keep `static/llms.txt` and `src/pages/ai.md` updated with any major career milestones or new consulting services.
